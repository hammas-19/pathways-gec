## ‚ú® Animation Guidelines

### Timing
- **Fast**: 150ms (micro-interactions)
- **Normal**: 300ms (standard transitions)
- **Slow**: 500ms (page transitions)
- **Very Slow**: 1000ms (hero animations)

### Easing
- **Default**: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- **In**: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 1, 1)
- **Out**: cubic-bezier(0, 0, 0.2, 1)
- **Bounce**: cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.55, 0.265, 1.55)

### Effects
- Fade in on scroll
- Slide up on scroll
- Scale on hover
- Lift on hover (elevation change)
- Gradient shifts
- Smooth page transitions

## üéØ Component Styles

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Gradient blue with hover lift
- **Secondary**: Outline with hover fill
- **Sizes**: Small (px-4 py-2), Medium (px-6 py-3), Large (px-8 py-4)
- **Radius**: Rounded-lg (8px)

### Cards
- **Shadow**: lg on normal, xl on hover
- **Radius**: Rounded-2xl (16px)
- **Padding**: p-6 to p-8
- **Hover**: Transform scale + shadow increase

### Forms
- **Inputs**: Rounded-lg, focus ring-2 ring-blue-500
- **Labels**: Font-medium, text-sm, text-slate-700
- **Validation**: Green for success, Red for error
